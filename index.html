Hi I'm a senior at Virginia Wesleyan University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Communications. In addition to my studies, I have been actively involved in a variety of extracurricular activities on campus.

Most notably, I served as a member of the executive boards for both Spectrum and Movie Crew. Spectrum is an organization that advocates for and supports the LGBTQ+ community, while Movie Crew is a group that coordinates and hosts movie events on campus. As part of these executive boards, I helped organize and execute various events and activities that brought the Virginia Wesleyan community together. These experiences taught me valuable leadership and teamwork skills that will be useful as I move forward in my career.

I am passionate about using technology to solve problems and enhance communication. This interest is reflected in my choice of major and minor. Outside of class, I enjoy exploring the latest advancements in technology and tinkering with gadgets.

As graduation approaches, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge to a career in the tech industry and make a meaningful impact.
